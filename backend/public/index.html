Tabchat

Tabchat is a browser extension that lets you leave comments on any webpage for others to see.

It's available now on Chrome. A Firefox version is coming soon!

And the best part? It's open source.

Privacy Policy

Tabchat is built to be privacy conscious and doesn't store any personally identifying information.

It uses Google's reCAPTCHA to prevent spam. Some of your behavior is sent to Google for it to analyze whether you're a bot or not.
